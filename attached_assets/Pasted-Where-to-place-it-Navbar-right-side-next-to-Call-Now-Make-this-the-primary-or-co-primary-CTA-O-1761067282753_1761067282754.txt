Where to place it

Navbar, right side, next to “Call Now”
Make this the primary or co-primary CTA. On large screens, show both “Call Now” and “Book Your Free In-Home Consultation.”

Hero section, above the fold
Mirror the same booking button in the hero so visitors see it immediately, even if your navbar collapses on some devices.

Mobile, sticky bottom bar
On small screens, convert to a 2-button sticky bar: “Call Now” and “Book Free Consult,” so users always have a thumb-reachable action.

Small but high-impact tweaks

Open the calendar link in a new tab and add rel="noopener" for performance and security.

Track clicks by appending UTM tags if you’re running ads:
?...utm_source=site&utm_medium=nav&utm_campaign=consults

Keep “Call Now” dark or neutral, and make “Book Free Consult” the warm gold to visually anchor the online option.
